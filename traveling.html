Athenas, Greece

This city can present by itself. The capital of Greece is a place with endless history on it. From the ancient greek culture, the Bizantine Era, all the way to our modern Oympic events.

The only downside about this amazing place is the huge amount of tourist that you will find. Also mind the temperatures that you will find in summer as it can get very hot.

Mtskheta, Georgia

Perhaps not one of the most popular countries in Europe but Georgia hides many beautiful scenaries all around its territory. From ancient castles all the way to incredible mountains and cities.

The Georgian territory and people are very welcoming, you can surely ask for a tip or help from an stranger in the streets. Another positive thing is that it is a cheap country and there are many different landscapes, you can find cities, beach and sea, an mountanous places.
